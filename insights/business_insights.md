# 📊 Customer Segmentation Insights

## 🔹 Key Findings

- High-value customers contribute a significant portion of total revenue
- A small percentage of customers drive majority of business sales
- Medium-value customers represent a strong opportunity for growth
- Low-value customers have low engagement and low spending

## 🔹 Business Recommendations

- Focus marketing efforts on high-value customers to retain them
- Target medium-value customers with offers to convert them into high-value
- Use discounts and campaigns to activate low-value customers
- Personalization strategies can increase overall revenue

## 🔹 Conclusion

Customer segmentation helps identify where revenue is coming from and enables better business decisions for growth and retention.
